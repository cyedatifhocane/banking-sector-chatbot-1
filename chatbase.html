Account Management

This website would enable users to perform various actions related to their bank accounts, such as checking balances, reviewing transaction history, transferring funds between accounts, setting up alerts for specific transactions, and managing account preferences like notification settings or account security options.

Critical Occupation List

This page would provide access to a chatbot interface or FAQ section where users can seek assistance with common banking inquiries or issues. The chatbot could offer responses to frequently asked questions, guide users through troubleshooting processes, and escalate more complex inquiries to human representatives if necessary. Additionally, it could offer support for services like card replacement, account closure, or reporting fraudulent activity.

Employment Visa

This page could offer information and access to various financial products and services offered by the bank, such as loans, mortgages, credit cards, investment options, and insurance products.
